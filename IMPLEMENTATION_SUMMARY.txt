Date: 2025-11-08
Status: COMPLETED
Module: GolferCaddyBooking (Standalone Caddy Booking for Golfers)

===============================================================================
WHAT WAS IMPLEMENTED
===============================================================================

A complete, standalone caddy booking system that allows golfers to:
- Browse caddies by golf course
- Filter caddies by rating, experience, language, and specialty
- View detailed caddy profiles
- Book caddies for specific dates and tee times
- View and manage their caddy bookings
- Cancel pending bookings

This is a FULL-PAGE SYSTEM that works independently of the tee time booking 
flow, specifically designed for golfers who already have tee times.

===============================================================================
WHAT THIS MODULE DOES NOT DO
===============================================================================

Out of Scope (by design):
- Does NOT manage caddy profiles (Golf Course Admin does this)
- Does NOT approve/confirm bookings (admin confirms)
- Does NOT handle payments (can be added later)
- Does NOT send notifications (can be added later)
- Does NOT integrate with tee time bookings (separate systems)
- Is NOT the Personal Caddy Organizer (separate agent task)
