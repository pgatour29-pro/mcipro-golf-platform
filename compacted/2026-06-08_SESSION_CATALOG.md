## Registration crash on iPhone Safari (ebebe722)
Pete: new person's dashboard won't load on Safari. Fix: multiplex onto ONE shared channel (`_eventChannel` + `_eventCallbacks`; repeat callers just push a callback); reset in `unsubscribeAll`. See [[realtime-channel-reuse-crash]].
