# MyCaddiPro Video Intro Script

## Opening

> *"Let me ask you a few questions about your current golf course software..."*

---

## Questions

**Question 1:**
> *"Can your golfers book a caddy at 9 o'clock at night?"*

**Question 2:**
> *"How are tee times confirmed the day before? Is someone on your staff making those calls?"*

**Question 3:**
> *"What happens when they don't pick up?"*

**Question 4:**
> *"When a tee time changes, how does the golfer find out? How does the society organizer find out?"*

**Question 5:**
> *"Can your caddies, golfers, caddy master, pro shop, and management all see the same information at the same time?"*

**Question 6:**
> *"Are they communicating through your system... or through personal LINE groups and phone calls?"*

---

## Close

> *"How many of those did you answer 'No' to?"*

---

## Shorter 20-Second Version

> *"Can your golfers book a caddy at 9 PM?*
> *Who's calling to confirm tee times tomorrow?*
> *What happens when they don't answer?*
> *When something changes, how does everyone find out?*
>
> *How many 'No's was that?"*

---

## Visual Suggestions

| Question | Visual |
|----------|--------|
| 9 PM booking | Clock showing 9:00 PM, phone screen |
| Staff calling | Frustrated staff member on phone |
| No answer | Phone ringing, unanswered |
| Real-time notifications | Phone ping animation |
| Connected communication | Network diagram showing all stakeholders |

---

## Implementation

The intro slide is implemented in `golf-platform-demo.tsx`:
- Auto-advances every 3 seconds
- Questions fade in sequentially
- Progress dots show current position
- "Skip to End" button available
- "See What MyCaddiPro Can Do" button appears after all questions
